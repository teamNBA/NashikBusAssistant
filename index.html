About Us

We are the students of computer engineering department from K. K. Wagh Institute of Engineering Education & Research, Nashik. For commuting on a daily basis within the city many of our friends use public transport in Nashik. However, they regularly had an inconvenient public transport experience.


Hence, we came up with this idea of developing an app which will help everyone to use the city bus services as per their convenience. Our goal is to help in providing a convenient public transport experience to every commuter of Nashik.
